The Chicago Family Housing Community (CFHC) is a six-unit building at 3816 W North Ave.

We are a community that:

  • welcomes children into each others' units to play
  • shares childcare, both informally (e.g. your child comes over to play with mine) and formally (e.g. I babysit for you when you have class Monday nights, and you babysit for me when I have band Thursdays)
  • has shared communal space in the basement and backyard
  • plans community events, like building-wide dinners and block parties
  • is intentionally inclusive of people of different races, classes, genders, sexual orientations, and other social identities

CFHC is a co-op. This means:

  • all residents of the co-op are its owners
  • we work together to maintain common spaces and the building
  • monthly payments go towards the building's mortgage and expenses
  • if you move out, you can get back your payments towards the mortgage principal
  • when someone leaves, the other residents decide who will move in
